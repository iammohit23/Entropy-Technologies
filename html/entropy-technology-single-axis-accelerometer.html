Single Axis MEMS Accelerometer

Entropy Technologies Accelerometers are an Excellent Choice for Low Noice,
Low Drift and Low Power.

Single Axis
Accelerometer.

This accelerometer is not ratio-metric to supply voltage, where bias and scale factor are depends on power supply variation. It operates with dual power supply where bias and scale factor are independent from power supply variation.

Download Data-sheet

Features :

  • 1. Tri axial configuration.
  • 2. Robust up to 5000g Acceleration (any axis, 0.1msec).
  • 3. Operate from dual Power Supply, offers excellent Bias Stability.
  • 4. Capacitive Sensor Operating Temperature Range -40'C to +125'C.
  • 5. Tunable Band Width from DC to 1KHz.
  • 6. Integrated Temperature Sensor.
  • 7. Full differential sensor and circuitry for high resistance to EMI/RFI.

Applications :


 
1. Inertial Measurement Unit (IMUs).
2. Altitude and Heading Reference System(AHRSs).
3. Platform Stabilization System Structural Health Monitoring Seismic Imaging Tilt sensing.
4. Vibration Measurement.
